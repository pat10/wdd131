About me

I'm Robert Patten. I was born and raised in central California and am the oldest of five children. My dad was an optometrist, and my mom was a music teacher. I served a Spanish-speaking mission in New Jersey, United States. I have a wife and two children. Coding is one of my creative outlets. The others are music and creative writing.
